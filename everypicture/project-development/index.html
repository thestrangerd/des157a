— Interaction Change —

back to portal page

For my Every Picture Project, I am creating a storybook about my childhood stuffed animals. To enhance user interaction with the site than simply flipping through the book pages, users will be able to type a word to "complete" the story. On each page, there will be a blank form. After typing the correct word (using context clues and a word key), the stuffed animal image will then animate onto the page. This way, users will more directly interact with the story unfolding.
